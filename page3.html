Prayer Walk

10. Christchurch Hospital We pray for healing that people will get better and know that God is with them through their struggles. We pray for the people whose lives are coming to an end on earth with believe that there is life after death and accept eternal life.
11. Christchurch Botanic Gardens We thank God for the beautiful world he has created, all the plants and animals.
12. Canterbury Museum We thank you for the knowledge we have of the past and pray that we will be able to use that knowledge to build a better future.
13. Te Pae Christchurch Convention Centre We pray that people are welcomed to our city and can see the beautiful place that God has made.
