Prayer Walk

This prayer walk was designed by the Junior Soldier Crest Group, from Christchurch City Salvation Army, to complete their corporate prayer section in their crest booklet.
On this website there is a map of our city, Christchurch. On it there is a path that leads you to 13 different places around our city which we have chosen for the prayer walk. Each place is colour coded and labelled with its corresponding prayer. For each prayer there is also an audio version if you would prefer to hear the prayer read.

Total prayer walk time: 1 hour and 25 min (approx)

Alternatively you may prefer to do the walk in three parts.

Section A (Prayers 1-5): 27 min
Section B (Prayers 5-10):30 min
Section C (Prayers 10-13): 28 min
