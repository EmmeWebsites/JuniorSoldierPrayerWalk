Prayer Walk

6. Christchurch Bus Interchange Life is a journey just like a bus ride, we have to choose weather we’re going to get on God’s bus or our own. We pray that we will make wise decisions on the bus we take.
7. The Crossing We pray that people focus on Godly things and not materialistic possessions. We pray that people remember that where you treasure is your heart will be also (Mathew 6:21)
8. Riverside Market We pray that people have happy and healthy interactions with each other.
9. Christchurch District Court We pray that the courts are a rehabilitating place and that people who have offended don’t do it again.
